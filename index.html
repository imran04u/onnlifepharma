About us

Established in 2006, ONN LIFE PHARMA, began operations in sales & marketing of pharmaceuticals products.

In pursuit of excellence, the group is forging ahead with its expertise and experience, constantly laying emphasis on customer satisfaction and confidence through quality control and assurance. A competent, dedicated and dynamic team of professionals are responsible for its aggressive growth.

ONN LIFE PHARMA has always strived to achieve quality & affordable products to help people lead healthier lives. The products we market go through quality checks & stringent standards of stability, bio-equivalence & clinical testing. ONN LIFE PHARMA an Indian pharmaceuticals company that develops, and markets modern branded generic pharmaceutical products.

ONN LIFE PHARMA is marketing pharmaceuticals formulations of a wide range of Tablets, Capsules, Liquids, Drops, Dry Syrups, Injectables, Dermatology, Powders, Ayurvedics. Products of ONN LIFE PHARMA are intended for the pain treatment disorders of central nervous system, digestive system, urinary tract, genital system & treatment of respiratory diseases.

ONN LIFE PHARMA we take care that all products surpass the stringiest of quality parameters. The company always strives to keep committed schedule. Company believes in QUALITY PRODUCTS, Reasonable price, prompt communication and quick delivery.
